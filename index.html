Diving into the jump of sugar consumption and the increase of diabetes cases

How has our consumption shifted? How much have diabetes increased, and what are the dangers with it?

We eat and drink more sweets than before.

Show for years: 1960-2018

Are we eating too much sugar?

Spoiler alert: yes we are!

More and more people get treated for diabetes.

And sugar is one major cause of diabetes type 2.

...and diabetes can harm us in many ways!

So what?

To reduce the risk of diabetes, and to comply with the recommendations, we should on average halve our daily sugar consumption. A nation-wide bisection could lead to a reduction in future diabetes cases. There are more causes of diabetes and sugar is not the only problem, but it's a start.
